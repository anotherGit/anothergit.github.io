ANOTHER JAY

Match-4 to survive real-time battles in pixelated, rogue-like adventures

Dive into the depths of dynamic dungeons and enchanted environments in Match And Rogue a unique blend of rogue-like adventure and real-time match-4 mechanics, all showcased in captivating pixel art design accompanied by 8-bit music and sound effects.

INNOVATIVE GAMEPLAY

Match And Rogue introduces a fresh take on the rogue-like genre with an innovative match-4 combat system. Players navigate through diverse dungeons each hosting procedurally generated rooms. Among these, combat rooms are where the real action unfolds, with enemies attacking in real time. Your arsenal? A 6x6 grid filled with various powers, from sword hits to freezing waves. Align four identical powers to unleash your attack, continuously strategizing to survive the onslaught.

VARIOUS ACTIVITIES

Your journey is peppered with oases of calm amidst the chaos. Restore your hero's life at a fireplace, fish for resources, mine for metal shards, or trade and shop for crucial items and passive gear that influence your stats.

CHARACTER PROGRESSION

At the heart of each level lies a village, a sanctuary where you can upgrade your hero's abilities using resources gathered on your adventure. Death is not the end but a chance to grow; earn XP for each enemy vanquished, invest in a skill tree, and return stronger, ready to venture deeper into the adventure.

PLAY WITH ONE HAND

Designed with the mobile player in mind, Match And Rogue uses portrait orientation, making it easy to play anytime, anywhere.

FEATURES

  • Unique blend of match-4, real-time combat and rogue-like gameplay
  • Procedurally generated levels ensure a new experience every time
  • Rich non-combat activities to enhance your journey
  • Deep character progression with a variety of powers and items
  • RPG mechanics, meet the NPCs who will help you on your quest
  • Nostalgic pixel art aesthetics with captivating 8-bit music
